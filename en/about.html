Company Introduction

Hangzhou Beneware Medical Equipment Co.,Ltd is a high-tech enterprises based on Zhejiang University for the technical and personnel support, specializing in medical equipment industry, set research and development, production, sales and service. Our company has been awarded by the German authority certification of ISO9001 & ISO13485 & CE certificate auditted by TUV.

Now our company has a highly efficient professional R & D team, 70% has a doctoral degree and 30% has a master's degree from biomedical engineering colleage of Zhejiang University. The most grateful is that our team's core technology has been successfully applied in the national space project. Based on the technology of the "astronauts physiological parameters of the monitoring system" was used in the "Tiangong No.1" travel space to monitor the astronauts physiological parameters.

Our products was made under the idea of "high-end design in technology, strict quality control in production", we insist to use advanced devices, pay attention to every step and details, use the aerospace standard in design, production and testing. The indicators are currently in the international advanced level. Young Reiz excellent talent, determined to build China and even world-class medical electronic equipment, to achieve "for more people to provide quality medical care," the company's vision.
